Personal work

>In this project, I am mainly responsible for the environment construction and adaptation of VR glasses and hand motion capture, the realization of the function of writing on paper with a pen, and the production of PPT. My teammates and I have battled with the equipment for many times. From the unfamiliar at the beginning to the distressingly skilled later, I was faced and managed to solve problems such as inability to connect, poor signal, and inability to charge.

> For the setting of the environment, we mainly rely on the steamVR plug-in supported by Unity and the HI5-2 interactive SDK user manual. The steamVR plugin can be downloaded and imported directly from the Unity store. The HI5-2 Interactive SDK User Manual introduces the project, scene, object and button settings of the HI5-2 Interactive SDK application in detail, and provides related interfaces. The process of writing is mainly divided into several processes: first, touch and pick up the pen with the hand; second, the tip of the pen touches the test paper; the third test paper presents handwriting. First imitate the example program, set the pen as an object that can be interacted by HI5-2, and realize the grasping and placement of the pen. Second, set the starting point of the ray on the tip of the pen. A ray is generated at the start point at the beginning of the program, in the direction of the pen tip. When the object touched by the ray is the testing paper, the coordinates of the touch are passed to the script of the test paper, and the test paper determines whether it is writing at this time. If the writing speed is fast, the missing pixels are supplemented in the left side of the two movements. At the same time, a reset button is set, which is convenient for the pen to return to its original position after being dropped.

> Finally, I recorded a video of the project results, edited a little and made a PPT;
